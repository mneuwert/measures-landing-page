Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

Introduction

Neuwert Media ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Measures mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

Measures is designed to work completely offline and does not require any personal information to function. The app stores your preferences and conversion history locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic usage analytics (crash reports, performance metrics) through Apple's built-in analytics
  • App Store analytics for download and usage statistics
  • No personal identifiers are collected or transmitted

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand general usage patterns to enhance features
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

All your conversion data, preferences, and favorites are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. This data is never transmitted to our servers or third parties. Currency exchange rates are fetched from public APIs but no personal information is sent with these requests.

Third-Party Services

Currency Exchange Rates

Real-time currency exchange rates are provided by third-party financial data services. These requests are anonymous and do not include any personal information.

Apple Services

Our app uses Apple's standard frameworks and services, which are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. This includes analytics, crash reporting, and App Store functionality.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All app data is under your control on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app functions entirely offline after initial currency rate downloads.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@neuwert-media.com
  • Through the App Store review system

Summary

Measures prioritizes your privacy by design. The app works completely offline, stores all data locally on your device, and only connects to the internet for anonymous currency rate updates. We collect no personal information and have no user accounts or tracking.
